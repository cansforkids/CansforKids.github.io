Volunteer

Volunteer activities include reaching out to social platforms, fundraising, collecting food, and delivering to the food bank. All activities can be done based on your own pace and are safe during this pandemic. In the future, we plan to organize groups of students to volunteer at Second Harvest Food Bank.

1. Student participants sign up through our official email cansforkidsnc@gmail.com
2. Each participant collects canned foods and sends the collected cans to a designated collecting location where all the cans are sorted by Cans for Kids. Receipts will be provided
3. Cans for Kids will determine the volunteer hours based on the weight of cans that each student collects
4. Each participant who donates cans will fill in the form on X2VOL on Naviance for Cans for Kids to approve
5. Cans for Kids will deliver all the collected foods to the food bank and keep receipts for records

Guidelines:

15lbs or more (minimum)

1 hour

30lbs or more

2 hours

45lbs or more

3 hours

60lbs or more

4 hours

75lbs or more

5 hours

90lbs or more

5 hours

Each student who donates will receive a receipe each time he/she donates. We will check with our records and approve hours based on the information above in x2VOL.
