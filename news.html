Cans for Kids January Event

On January 20th, Cans for Kids donated hundreds of pounds of food to the Community House Middle School in order to help support build the pantry being built there by working with Ballantyne Helping Neighbors in Need. The food was first collected over the course of 2 months, then sorted by a group of volunteers before being brought to the pantry and sorted. This pantry is designed to bring aid to those in CHMS and their families who are in need of food.

Southwest Middle School Donation

This November's volunteer event ended in great success. We donated over 200 books and had 12 volunteers who participated.

October Yard Sale Fundraiesr

This October's yard sale fundraiser had over 15 volunteers helping out. We sold a variety of items that included toys, books, and clothes. In total, we raised over 400 dollars for our Second Harvest Food Bank.
