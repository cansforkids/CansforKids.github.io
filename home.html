About Cans for Kids


Cans for Kids is a project recently created by four high school students. We work to collect canned goods from our community and donate them in order to help feed children that do not have enough food in their household. This project was specifically started during the Covid-19 pandemic to aid families that may have lost their jobs during these troubling times. By starting this project, we hope to make our community a better place for everyone by donating to those in need of help.

Our goals


Cans for Kids was founded as a way to bring attention to the many students who are struggling because of COVID-19. In the wake of the pandemic, already impoverished children are unable to find food because their parents are cannot go to work. Cans for Kids aims to help. We collect your donations of food and money to benefit the youths of our community.

Cans for Kids has also donated masks to workers who provide food for those in need.
So far, 600 masks have been donated.
