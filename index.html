CANS FOR KIDS

About Us

Cans for Kids is a nonprofit organization created by four high school students in April of 2020. Since the Covid-19 pandemic began, Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools (CMS) has distributed millions of meals to their students in need. To contribute to these efforts, we decided to collect canned goods and drinks from members of our community and donate it to help students who do not have enough food in their homes, as well as to the families that may have lost their jobs during these troubling times.

Our Mission

Cans for Kids aims to aid children who have been less fortunate during the Covid-19 pandemic by helping them get daily meals. In addition to collecting food and drink donations, we provide volunteer opportunities to CMS students during our food drives and fundraisers. Through these efforts, we hope to encourage other students to step forward and make our community a better place.

Cans for Kids has donated masks to workers who provide food for those in need.

So far, 300 masks have been donated to CMS workers who distribute food to students, and another 300 to Second Harvest Food Bank workers.

Cans for Kids has achieved so much with the help of every volunteer.

As of March 2021, we have organized over 20 food drives, donated more than 6,000 pounds of food to the local food bank, recruited over 80 volunteers, and contributed more than 2,000 volunteer hours to the effort in helping children in need.
