CANS FOR KIDS

About Us

Cans for Kids was initiated by Jerry and Richard Lin with two other Ardrey Kell high school students Joshua and Eric Cai in March of 2020. Since the Covid-19 pandemic began, Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools (CMS) has distributed millions of meals to students in need. To contribute to these efforts, we collect non-perishable foods from our community and donate it to help students during these troubling times. We officially became a school club in October of 2021 and we continue to serve our community and provide volunteer opportunities to our students.

Our Mission

Cans for Kids aims to aid children who have been less fortunate during the Covid-19 pandemic by helping them get daily meals. In addition to collecting food and drink donations, we provide volunteer opportunities to CMS students during our food drives and fundraisers. Through our value of students supporting their peers, we raise awareness of the issues in our local community and encourage other students to step forward and make our community a better place.

Accomplishments

Cans for Kids has achieved so much with the help of every volunteer.

As of January 2022, we have organized over 45 food drives, donated almost 10,000 pounds of food to the local food bank, recruited over 100 volunteers, and contributed more than 2,500 volunteer hours to the effort in helping children in need.

Cans for Kids has donated masks to workers who provide food for those in need.

So far, 300 masks have been donated to CMS workers who distribute food to students, and another 300 to Second Harvest Food Bank workers. CMS reported this achievement on the CMS website.
