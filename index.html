About Cans for Kids


Cans for Kids is a nonprofit organization created by four high school students in April of 2020. Since the Covid-19 Pandemic began, Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools has distributed millions of meals to their students in need. After realizing this situation, we decided to collect canned food from people in their community and donate it to supply food for students who do not have enough of it in their households, and to the families that may have lost their jobs during these troubling times.

Our goals


Cans for Kids aims to raise awareness about the CMS students who are struggling to eat daily meals because of this Covid-19 outbreak and to encourage other CMS students to step forward to assist them. Through donations from students, we would like to help feed those less fortunate and make our community a better place to live.

Cans for Kids has also donated masks to workers who provide food for those in need.
So far, 600 masks have been donated.

As of March 2021, we have donated more than 4000 pounds of food and recruited over 30 volunteers.




Donate to Cans for Kids now to support families in need.

All donations will be used to purchase needed canned food.
