HIGHLIGHTS

April 17th, 2020

Cans for Kids starts operations with our first donation to Second Harvest Food Bank after collecting over 100 pounds of cans, pasta, and other non-perishable goods.

May, 2020

Cans for Kids donates 300 masks to Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools to assist in the fight against the Covid-19 pandemic along with another 300 masks to Second Harvest Food Bank.

Summer 2020

Cans for Kids members volunteer in-person at Second Harvest Food Bank, helping to pack food and sort donations over the course of the whole summer.

October 31st, 2020

Cans for Kids hosts an in-person food drive at Stone Creek Ranch Community Clubhouse, gathering donations, weighing food, and sorting food. Cans for Kids advertised through flyers and emails in the weeks leading up to the event. There were 15 volunteers that helped out, and over 500 pounds were collected.

March 27th, 2021

Cans for Kids hosts an in-person food drive at Stone Creek Ranch Community Clubhouse. 21 volunteers participated in this food drive by gathering donations, weighing food, and sorting food. Cans for Kids advertised through flyers and emails leading up to the event. Over 800 pounds of food were collected during this food drive.
